2FA (Two Factor Authentication) is one of the most effective measures / choices that can be taken in order to mitigate both phishing and spear phishing attacks. 2FA alone is insufficient to gain entry. Strict password management strategies can be used along with the 2FA as these together prevent the use of compromised credentials. According to “Markus Jakobsson, Agari” 2FA has become one of the most widely accepted back-up verifications for many businesses and services.

According to “Lily Hay Newman, Wired” phishing is a major threat, but awareness of the consequences it has is beneficial as there are many ways to defend yourself. Always consider the source as phishers will always make their source look like it is from a legitimate source, knowing where an email comes from is important. Make sure each password is different, as if a phisher has one of your passwords it will be harder for them to know all your passwords, alongside using 2FA. Knowing the ways to keep yourself safe online and away from hackers, is crucial in the times when technology is continuously evolving.

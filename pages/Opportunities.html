Once an offender is caught committing identity theft it creates chances for educating the offender. A study by Copes & Veterus (2009) states that identity thefts have a bounded rationality. A URL based approach can be used to prevent any further increase in phishing. It uses page rank and combines it with other metrics derived on URL knowledge. Phishing via emails: Problem
The problem with emails is that the user has no dependable way to prove the validity of emails from legitimate organizations which is why they often fall victim to phishing. Solution
An approach that could be taken to reduce this could be that the emails sent by legitimate organizations are required to digitally sign off the email that they send so that the user is able to verify the contents of the email and reduce the chance of it to be a fake hence reducing the chance for it to be phished.
